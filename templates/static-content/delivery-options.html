Option 1: QuickTime Movie File

Accepted codecs:

ProRes4444 | ProRes422 HQ | ProRes422 | Avid DNxHD

Audio can be embedded in the QuickTime file, or be supplied as discrete mono channels of 24-bit 48 kHz uncompressed audio in WAV format.

Option 2: Image Sequence

Accepted image formats:

TIFF | DPX

Images must be placed in a single folder named "Sequence" and should be named sequentially.

Audio must be delivered as discrete mono channels of 24-bit 48 kHz uncompressed audio in WAV format.

Option 3: Tape

Accepted tape formats:

HDCAM | HDCAM-SR | DVCPROHD | XDCAM

Audio track assignment must be clearly labeled.

Only advanced 23.98 fps pulldown is accepted.
