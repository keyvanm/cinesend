Resolution and Frame Rate

Your picture should not be pillar or letter-boxed. The image should fill the entire frame. HD Content it padded to fit a Flat container.

Acceptable source image resolutions include: 2K Flat (1998×1080) | 2K Scope (2048×858) | HD (1920×1080)

Acceptable source frame rates: 24fps | 23.98fps | 25fps | 30fps | 29.97fps

Delivering file based masters

You must ensure that your audio and video are synchronous. While we don’t require any leader, we strongly advise that you include a 2-pop at the start of your files.

Files must be delivered on a USB3 or Thunderbolt drive formatted in either HFS, HFS+ or NTFS, and contain ONLY the final delivery elements. We cannot guarantee the integrity of the data on your drive, so we strongly advise that you do NOT send your only copy.

We will make every effort to return hard drives in their original packaging, however we may need to substitute our own packaging if the box you supply does not meet our shipping requirements.

How to ship your media

Please make sure your hard drive or tape is properly packaged for transit. Damaged media will delay the creation of your DCP.
